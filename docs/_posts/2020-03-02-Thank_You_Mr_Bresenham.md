Thank you [Jack Elton Bresenham](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jack_Elton_Bresenham) for the [line algorithm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bresenham%27s_line_algorithm). It translates nicely into ASCII visualizations and thereby can be used in all sorts of text-based contexts e.g in the drawing tool [SketchASCIIDrawingApplication](https://github.com/dennisppaul/skizzenbuch/tree/master/SketchASCIIDrawingApplication).
